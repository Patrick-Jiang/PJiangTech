Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers for businesses establishing their digital foundation.

How do you ensure the work is performed to professional standards?

Every project and service engagement begins with a detailed Statement of Work (SOW). This document clearly defines the scope, technical requirements, and expected deliverables, ensuring full transparency. By following a structured, ITIL-aligned process, we guarantee that all infrastructure changes are documented, tested, and executed with zero surprise to your operations.

What IT services do you provide for small businesses?

We handle everything from network setup and managed IT services to cloud solutions, server management, email, and hardware support—all tailored for small teams.

Do I need to hire a full-time IT person?

Not necessarily. Patrick Jiang provides managed IT services that cover all your IT needs at a fraction of the cost of hiring full-time staff.

How much does it cost to start IT for a small business?

Costs vary depending on requirements. We focus on low-cost, practical solutions like cloud setups, Proxmox virtualization, and affordable software to minimize expenses.

What is Proxmox and how can it help my small business?

Proxmox allows us to run multiple virtual servers on one physical server, saving hardware costs, simplifying backups, and increasing flexibility for your infrastructure.

Can you set up my office network securely?

Yes. We install RJ45 cabling, configure switches, and deploy WiFi access points with enterprise security so your network is fast, reliable, and safe from day one.

Can you help onboard new employees?

Absolutely. We set up email, install software, configure permissions, and ensure each employee can start working immediately without technical friction.

Do you protect my business from cyberattacks?

We implement a security-first methodology: firewalls, antivirus, secure WiFi, and remote monitoring to detect and resolve threats before they impact your business.

How quickly do you respond if something breaks?

We specialize in rapid response, often resolving critical infrastructure problems within an hour to minimize downtime for your team.
